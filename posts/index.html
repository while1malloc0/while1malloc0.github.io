Navigating Technical Disagreement

In Never Split the Difference: Negotiating As If Your Life Depended On It, Chris Voss writes that getting someone to say “no” is the start of a productive negotiation. Pushing for a hard “yes” can alienate and annoy people. “No” allows us to find hidden points of contention, and clarify what we actually want.
